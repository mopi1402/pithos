## Scope

This demo uses a curated subset of ~30 Pokémon, not the full National Pokédex.
The goal is to illustrate the Iterator design pattern (next(), pagination, tree
traversal, exhaustion), not to build a complete Pokédex. Keeping the dataset
small also keeps the repository lightweight (~1 MB of sprites).
